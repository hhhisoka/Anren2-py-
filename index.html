About RPG WhatsApp Bot

How to Play

Our RPG WhatsApp bot allows players to create characters, battle monsters, complete quests, and interact with other players through WhatsApp.

Getting Started
  1. Add our WhatsApp number to your contacts
  2. Start a conversation with "help" to see available commands
  3. Create your character with "register [username] [race] [class] [element]"
  4. Begin your adventure!

Features

  • Character Creation: Choose from 5 races, 14 classes, and 7 elements
  • Combat System: Battle other players in duels or face monsters
  • Progression: Level up and advance through 9 ranks from G to SS
  • Economy: Earn gold, buy items, and manage inventory
  • Exploration: Visit different zones with unique effects
  • Quests: Complete missions for rewards
  • Deity System: Special powers for admins and promoted players
